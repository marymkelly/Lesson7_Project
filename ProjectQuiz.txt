Question #4: (10 points)
++++++++++++
What is a Content Delivery Network (CDN)?  List and explain at least FOUR advantages in using a CDN.

A CDN is "is an interconnected system of cache servers that use geographical proximity as a criteria for delivering Web content."
Some advantages of them include that content will get to the client faster, they precache files so your site
won't need to redownload all the content when revisiting a page, they have built-in version control,
and analytics which helps the owner be able to monitor what is and isn't popular on their site.
